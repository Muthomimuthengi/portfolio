About Me

I am Pastor Duncan Muthomi, a devoted Adventist pastor with a passion for spreading the gospel and serving the community. My journey in faith is dedicated to spiritual guidance, pastoral care, and fostering a welcoming environment for all to experience God's love.

With a strong foundation in theological studies and a heart for ministry, I strive to deliver sermons that inspire and provide meaningful Bible studies. My goal is to support individuals and families in their spiritual walk and make a positive impact in our community.
